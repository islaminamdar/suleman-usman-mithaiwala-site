Our Story

We don't just make sweets — we craft traditions.

The family legacy began in 1926, when Sulemanbhai, a passionate young bakery assistant from Poona, opened his first sweet shop in Bombay. Over the decades, an unwavering commitment to quality, authenticity and taste made the family name a household word across India and beyond.

The legacy was carried forward by his son Abdul Gafoor Suleman Usman Mithaiwala — the second generation — whose dedication ensured the taste, the recipes and the trust remained unchanged.

Today, his grandson — the third generation — has built this chain of twelve branches across India, the UAE and Saudi Arabia, carrying the family name and the original recipes to new cities, year after year.
